About Me

Vassilis was born in Athens, Greece at 1990 and he is currently living in Ioannina city. He aquired his Integrated MSc degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Ioannina, Greece at 2019.

Alongside with his studies, he was the chairman of IEEE University of Ioannina Student Branch as a volunteer for 3 years, where he organized seminars, workshops and introduced the Arduino board and its possibilities. Furthermore, he worked as a freelancer for 5+ years creating several projects by scratch.

He work as a Front-End Developer with the latest frameworks like Laravel and Angular and as a part-time Freelancer maintaining websites in Wordpress platform.

He has published two technical books about Drones and IOT in collaboration with Packt Publishing, UK. Thus he has gained quite strong writing and communication skills.
